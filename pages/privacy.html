Privacy Policy

Data Collection

ToolsBoXMachine is hosted on Vercel. To ensure the stability, performance, and security of our service, Vercel automatically collects and stores log data when you visit this site. This information includes:

  • Your Public IP Address
  • Approximate Location (Country, City)
  • Your Operating System (OS)
  • Your User Agent (Browser type and version)
  • Referrer URL and requested pages

This data is used solely for infrastructure monitoring and security purposes.

Client-Side Processing

Security is our priority. Unlike most online utilities, all tools available on TBXM are executed entirely within your browser.

None of the data you process (images you blur, passwords you generate, or text you correct) is ever sent to our servers. Everything stays on your local machine, ensuring total privacy and maximum security.

Cookies

We use local storage only to remember your preferences (like the last tool used) for a better user experience. No tracking cookies are used.
